Our Goals

Encouraging students to participate in student and community activities in the fields related to architecture, environment, and arts

Promoting cooperation and guidance through joint work among students, educational institutions, and experts

Providing students with the knowledge and necessary skills to make use of after graduation

Launching a platform on social media to raise awareness and spread architectural content and activities
